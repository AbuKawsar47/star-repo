The Daily Star

21 January 2019

Ali Ahmed decimated South Point

Sports Reporter


A blistering hundred from Abu Kawsar helped Ali Ahmed school and College post a mammoth 439-run total against South Point School and College and subsequently register a huge 283-run win in the Prime Bank Young Tiger School Cricket tournament yesterday.

Electing to bat, Abu Kawsar struck a 98-ball 167-run knocke laced with 24 fours and 8 sixes while Abdullah Al Jubair added a quickie-fire 39-ball 77-run as Ali Ahmed posted 439-run for eight in 50 overs.

In reply South Point were all-out on just 156-runs in 24.5 overs.
